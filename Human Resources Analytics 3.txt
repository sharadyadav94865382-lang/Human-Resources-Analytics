======================================================================================
POWER BI INTERACTIVE DASHBOARD STRUCTURE & VISUAL CONFIGURATION CODE
======================================================================================

[TAB 1: WORKFORCE OVERVIEW & RECRUITMENT]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. TOP KPI CARDS (Ribbon Panel)
   - Visual Type: Card (New) / Multi-row Card
   - Data Fields: 
     * Field 1: [Active_Workforce] (Format: Display unit -> None)
     * Field 2: [Turnover_Rate_Percent] (Format: Percentage -> 2 Decimal)
     * Field 3: [Avg_Satisfaction] (Format: Decimal -> 1 Decimal)

2. DEPARTMENT HEADCOUNT DISTRIBUTION
   - Visual Type: Donut Chart / Pie Chart
   - Legend: final_hr_dataset[Department]
   - Values: [Active_Workforce]
   - Properties: Detail labels -> "Category, percent of total"

3. RECRUITMENT TREND OVER TIME
   - Visual Type: Stacked Column Chart
   - X-Axis: final_hr_dataset[Hire_Year] aur [Hire_Month_Name]
   - Y-Axis: [Total_Workforce]
   - Legend: final_hr_dataset[Status]


[TAB 2: PERFORMANCE & SATISFACTION INSIGHTS]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SATISFACTION VS SALARY CORRELATION
   - Visual Type: Scatter Chart
   - X-Axis: [Avg_Satisfaction]
   - Y-Axis: final_hr_dataset[Monthly_Salary] (Set to Average)
   - Legend/Play Axis: final_hr_dataset[Department]

2. PERFORMANCE RATING BREAKDOWN
   - Visual Type: Clustered Bar Chart
   - Y-Axis: final_hr_dataset[Performance_Rating]
   - X-Axis: [Total_Workforce]
   - Data Labels: On


[TAB 3: PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS & HIRING FORECAST]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. FUTURE HIRING REQUIREMENTS CARD
   - Visual Type: Card
   - Data Field: [Predictive_Hiring_Need]
   - Subtitle/Label: "Estimated Hires Required Next Month"

2. TIME SERIES FORECASTING MODEL (CRITICAL STEP)
   - Visual Type: Line Chart
   - X-Axis: final_hr_dataset[Hire_Date] (Right-click and change from Hierarchy to 'Hire_Date')
   - Y-Axis: [Total_Workforce]
   
   * HOW TO ENABLE MACHINE LEARNING FORECAST IN POWER BI:
     1. Click on the Line Chart visual.
     2. Go to the 'Format Visual' pane on the right.
     3. Click on the 'Analytics' tab (Magnifying Glass Icon).
     4. Scroll down to 'Forecast' and toggle it to 'ON'.
     5. Set Settings:
        - Forecast Length: 10 (Points or Months depending on axis)
        - Confidence Interval: 95%
        - Seasonality: Auto
     6. Click 'Apply'. 
        --> Power BI will automatically inject a shaded predictive band showing future hiring needs.
